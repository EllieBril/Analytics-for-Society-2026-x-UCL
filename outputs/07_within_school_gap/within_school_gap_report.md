# Within-School Gap Report

## Completion
- Status: fully completed.

## Design
- Schools are included only when at least 20 students are available and the within-school SES gap can be estimated.
- Performance split uses the sample median school mean maths of 445.5.
- Inequality split uses the sample median within-school gap of 28.4.

## Findings
- `High performance / High inequality`: mean maths 501.9, within-school gap 69.6, risk 59.6, negative climate -0.04.
- `High performance / Low inequality`: mean maths 511.6, within-school gap 0.8, risk 56.4, negative climate -0.31.
- `Low performance / High inequality`: mean maths 394.1, within-school gap 54.5, risk 46.4, negative climate -0.08.
- `Low performance / Low inequality`: mean maths 375.3, within-school gap -0.2, risk 41.8, negative climate -0.09.

## Interpretation rule
- Same-school inequality patterns show which school contexts are associated with more unequal outcomes among students in the same institution.
- They do not identify the causal reason for the gap.
